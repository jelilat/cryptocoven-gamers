Welcome to the Coven!

A witch is lost in the wilderness. Only you can help her find her way back.

Rules of the Game

1. Summon a Witch to reveal her face

2. You will be given 5 chances to say her name.

3. If you say her name correctly, you will be rewarded.

Anyone can summon a witch, but only those who say her name can take her home.
